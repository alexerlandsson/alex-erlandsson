About

I’m a front-end developer based in Gothenburg, Sweden, professionally active since 2015, but the spark ignited long before that. It all began in 2009 with a high school course in web development—and I’ve been immersed in the craft ever since. The deeper I go, the more I enjoy it.

My first real project was a website for a company I co-founded with classmates as part of the Young Enterprise (UF) program at high school. We sourced and sold portable solar-powered device chargers, and over two semesters, we iterated on several versions of our website. The final version won Best Website at the Ung Företagsamhet Älvsborg Fair in 2011. Looking back, that experience marked the true beginning of my professional path.

Although I briefly explored ERP systems at university, it was clear that the web was where I belonged. Not long after graduating, in 2015, I joined Our Studio – where I still work today. Over the years, I’ve been involved in projects for some of Sweden’s most well-known companies, building design systems and applications at scale.

I especially enjoy creating scalable design systems, with a strong focus on reusable components and accessibility (a11y).
